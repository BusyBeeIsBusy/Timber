Timber

Timber is a way for avid hikers, photographers, and nature lovers to discover local state parks. We have combined photography, nature, and technology to create a program that will take some work off of the prospective hiker’s back. By merging these ideas we hope to give the user the experience of finding what local, Indiana, state park to go to that they were missing before. In our research we found that hikers can get bored of their same parks and trails that they take and were open to the idea of exploring different parks. It can be time-consuming to research each park and find new trails based on their preferences so Timber was created to solve this problem.
